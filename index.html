Projects

Summary of the projects I have worked on.

Trade Tracker App

Trade Tracker App is a web application I developed to help you keep track of your 'options' trades in the stock market. It gives you the ability to plan, monitor and evaluate your contracts with a degree of efficiency to make investing a much easier process. Simply save your 'options' contract details and you can easily keep track of your trade contracts and their expiry with the help of the dashboard or by viewing individual contracts you can plan your exit points ahead and know when to sell your contracts to maximize your profit before they expire.

SMS Admin

SMS Admin is a revolutionary management system that provides online services to students, parents, teachers and the institution for academic cores and keeping track of students academic activities, progress and records. It is designed to support different standards of grading systems used in countries like the United States, Philippines and Australia. Moreover, the architecture implemented in SMS-Admin is flexible and scalable to support any academic level such as colleges and universities, high schools, elementary schools, primary schools and vocational schools.

Treasury Wine Estates - Grange2020

Treasury Wine Estates (TWE) is one of the world’s largest producers of quality wine, with a dedicated vision of becoming the world’s most celebrated wine company. I am proud to be involved in a project (Grange2020) to modernize and reengineer TWE's historic wine-making and operations system, replacing the front-end on a legacy system that has been in operation for 30 years.

Treasury Wine Estates - SWEET

SWEET is an intake management system, that provides visibility to the wineries on incoming loads of grapes. The solution includes a web application that functions much like an arrivals and departures board at an airport, detailing if trucks are running on time, early or late, and if there is more or less fruit than expected on board. And a mobile app that allows truck drivers to provide the wineries with their location on leaving the vineyard or via tracking capabilities on route. This in turn provides wineries with an accurate estimated time of arrival. This application has been built recently with Esri's AppStudio for ArcGIS, providing one set of code (QT/QML/JavaScript) to deploy native apps to both iOS and Android devices.
